Welcome to Annie's Pizzeria

Annie's is your go-to pizza spot at 213 Middlesex Ave, serving the Medford community with a stacked menu of familiar favorites and fun specialty combinations.

From classic cheese and build-your-own pies to signature creations loaded with toppings, there’s something for everyone. Round it out with fried dough, wings, subs, wraps, salads, calzones, tacos, and more.

Whether you’re grabbing a quick lunch, feeding the family, or ordering for a game night, Annie’s keeps it casual, comforting, and always delicious.
